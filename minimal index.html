Company Overview

Immunome AI Biotechnologies LLC is an early-stage company focused on multi-omics, cardiovascular immunology, and endotype discovery. We build Cytoseeker, a platform that integrates biobank-scale data with single-cell and proteomic measurements to identify disease endotypes, biomarkers, and therapeutic targets.

Current Focus Areas

  • Coronary artery disease and myocardial infarction risk stratification.
  • Integration of clinical, genetic, proteomic, and single-cell datasets.
  • Endotype discovery and minimal marker panels for translational PoC.
Multi-omics Cardiovascular Biobanks Endotypes AI / GNN / Transformers

Use of UK Biobank Data

As part of our early research program, we plan to use UK Biobank data to:

  • Construct UKBB-style cardiovascular cohorts with standard CVD outcomes.
  • Develop and evaluate endotype-driven risk models and minimal marker panels.
  • Benchmark Cytoseeker against established clinical baselines and polygenic risk scores.

Analyses will be conducted strictly within UK Biobank’s governance framework and used for methodological research and prototyping, not for direct clinical decision-making.
